Admission rules

At Holy Cross College of Management and Technology, our admission process is designed to identify and nurture promising talent. We seek students who demonstrate academic potential, leadership qualities, and a passion for making a meaningful impact. Our holistic approach considers not just academic achievements, but also personal growth, creativity, and commitment to excellence.

Admission requirements

We welcome ambitious students who are ready to transform their educational journey. Candidates must submit: High school transcripts with strong academic performance Standardized test scores (SAT/ACT) Personal statement highlighting personal achievements and aspirations Letters of recommendation Proof of English proficiency (for international students) Each application is reviewed comprehensively, ensuring we select students who align with our institution's values of innovation, social responsibility, and academic excellence.

Registration procedure

The admission process at Holy Cross is straightforward and student-friendly:

  1. Complete the online application form
  2. Submit required documents
  3. Pay the application fee
  4. Participate in an interview (virtual or on-campus)
  5. Await official communication regarding admission status

Our admissions team is committed to supporting you throughout the process, providing guidance and answering any questions you may have.

Financial aid

We believe that exceptional education should be accessible to all talented students. Holy Cross offers:

  • Merit-based scholarships
  • Need-based financial assistance
  • Work-study programs
  • External scholarship guidance
  • Flexible payment plans

Our financial aid office works closely with students to explore various funding options, ensuring that financial constraints do not hinder educational opportunities. We are dedicated to making your academic dreams achievable.
