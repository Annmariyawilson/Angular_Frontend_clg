Get More Than Holy Cross College

Holy Cross College of Management and Technology in Puttady, established in 2003, is a beacon of quality education in the Idukki District. Nestled amidst lush tea plantations, this picturesque campus offers a serene backdrop for learning. With a team of skilled instructors and efficient management, the college has garnered numerous accolades among M.G. University affiliates. It provides six undergraduate and three postgraduate programs, fostering a diverse student community from across India and beyond.

Campus Life

Holy Cross College features one of the best campus environments in Kerala Idukki with academic and extra circular activities. College has variety of Fests including Arts, Sports Fest, Interdepartmental and Intercollege Fest.

Clubs & Cells

Clubs include National Service Scheme (NSS), Language and Literacy clubs, Commerce Clubs, IT clubs, Dance and Theatre clubs. Also, college encompasses Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC), Placement and Carrer Guidance Cell, Skill Development Cells, Sports Club and Adventure Club.

Examination

Mahatma Gandhi University has introduced a comprehensive Four-Year Undergraduate Programme (FYUGP) starting from the 2024-2025 academic year. Degree program can be completed in 3 Years by getting 133 credits.

Facilities

College has a residential life with hostel accommodations for approximately 200+ students. College has a well-stocked library with eBooks, Football, Volleyball, Kabaddi and Badminton Courts.

About Holy Cross College

Established in 2003, Holy Cross College, Puttady offers high-quality undergraduate and postgraduate education. Known for its strong teaching staff and effective management, it has earned recognition among colleges affiliated with M.G. University.

Affiliations

Mahatma Gandhi University offers 250+ courses across various disciplines, enrolling approximately 150,000 students annually.

Distance Education

Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU), the world's largest university, provides diverse distance education programs.
