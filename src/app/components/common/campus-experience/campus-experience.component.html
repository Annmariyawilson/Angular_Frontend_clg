The campus experience

Holy Cross College offers a vibrant campus experience nestled among scenic tea plantations in Puttady. Students enjoy modern facilities, comfortable hostels with dedicated wardens, and a supportive community that fosters both academic excellence and personal growth in a pollution-free environment.

Our campus life includes numerous clubs, cultural events, and sports activities that complement the academic curriculum. With dedicated faculty mentors, 24/7 security services, and a peaceful atmosphere, students develop holistic skills while building lifelong connections in our inclusive and diverse community.
