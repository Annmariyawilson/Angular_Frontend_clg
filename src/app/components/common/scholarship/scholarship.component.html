Merit-Based Scholarship Programme

At Holy Cross College of Management and Technology, we offer a merit-based scholarship programme to support and reward academic excellence.

  • Students who have performed well in their higher secondary examinations are eligible for significant fee concessions.
  • Depending on their level of achievement, students can receive between 10% and 50% reduction in tuition fees.
  • Students securing top grades in all subjects are granted a full fee waiver, making their education completely free.

This initiative aims to encourage talented students to pursue higher education without financial barriers.
